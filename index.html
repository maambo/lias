About

Objectives

1. Public Campus Ministries aims to inspire, educate, equip and empower Seventh-day Adventists To be disiples of jesus and to share the everlasting gospel on campus, in the church and in the community.

2. PCM collaborates with Adventist Chaplaincy Ministries(ACM),GC Education Dept, Adventist Youth Ministries and the Adventist Health Ministries in providing support to Seventh-day Adventists who for variety of reasons attend non-Adventist private and public colleges and universties around the world.

In Cooperation with leader at various church levels. PCM endeavors to:

  • Strengthen the faith commitment of students to Seventh-day Adventist beliefs and mission
  • Prepare students to deal with the intellectual challenges that arise in a secular environment
  • Develop the Leadership abilities of students.
  • Provide students with opportunities for Christian fellowship.
  • Train students for outreach, mission, service and witness on campus, in the church and in the community.

Core Values

  • Character Over Competency
  • Collaboration Over Comppetion
  • Challenge Over Criticism

Aim/Vision

To transform students on non-Adventist campuses into Seventh-day Adventist ambassadors of christ in colleges/universties, churches, communties and the world at large

Mission

To inspire Seventh-day Adventist students to be disciples of Christ and empower them to share the everlasting gospel on campus.
